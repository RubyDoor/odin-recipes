Cheesecake

This is a comforting Cheesecake recipie, just like Grandma used to make! Also quick and simple, with just three Ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 125g plain sweet biscuits
  • 395g tin sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 packets of cream cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C fan forced.
  2. Grease and line a springform pan.
  3. Crush biscuits to fine crumbs.
  4. Add 2 tbspns of the sweetened condensed milk and stir to combine.
  5. Spoon into prepared pan and press over the base.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, using electric beaters, beat the remaining sweetened condensed milk with the cream cheese in a large bowl until smooth.
  8. Pour over biscuit base.
  9. Bake for 40 minutes.
  10. Refridgerate overnight before decorating with chopped fruit and serving.
