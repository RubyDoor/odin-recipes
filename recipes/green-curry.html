Green Curry

This is a spicy Thai Green Curry recipie, sure to tantalise your tastebuds!

Ingredients

  • 1 jar of green curry paste
  • 1 tin of coconut milk
  • 300ml water
  • 1 tbspn vegetable oil
  • 1 diced onion
  • 500g diced meat/poultry
  • 500g diced vegetables of choice
  • Fish sauce to taste
  • Basil leaves to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in frying pan and fry onions and meat until browned.
  2. Add vegetables and cook while stirring until slightly softened.
  3. Add curry paste, cook until fragrant.
  4. Add coconut cream and water.
  5. Cover and simmer until all ingredinets are cooked.
  6. Add fish sauch to taste.
  7. Finish with some basil leaves.
  8. Serve with fluffy Jasmine rice
