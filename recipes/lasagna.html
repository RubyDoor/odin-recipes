Lasagna

This is a delicious minced beef lasagna, with a delightfully rich cheesy sauce. Sure to become a family favourite!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbspn vegetable oil
  • 300g sliced mushrooms
  • 1 diced onion
  • 500g beef mince
  • 500g jar spaghetti sauce
  • 2 packets of fresh lasagna noodles
  • 500g jar bechamel sauce
  • 2 cups grated mozzarella cheesse
  • small packet of grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C fan forced.
  2. Heat oil in frying pan and fry onions and mushrooms.
  3. Add mince and cook while stirring until browned.
  4. Add spaghetti sauce.
  5. Spoon a little sauce into the bottom of a baking dish and spread evenly.
  6. Cover with a layer of lasagna noodles.
  7. Add a layer of the meat sauce, then bechamel sauce, then cover with mozzarella.
  8. Top with a layer of lasagna noodles and continue to layer with remaining meat sauce, bechamel sauce and mozzarella.
  9. Finish with the bechamel sauch and mozzarella, and sprinkle with parmesan.
  10. Bake for 40 minutes. Stand for 10 minutes before serving.
